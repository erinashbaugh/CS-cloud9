Self-Defence in a School

District Letter

Over 750,000 students in the U.S. from age 12-18 have experienced a crime. Many of these students have little to no self-defense knowledge and because of this, an attacker sees them as an easy target, also an attacker looks for a person with low self esteem who is more likely to give up in an attack. I propose a self defense class can lead to the safety of many kids. This class can be either an Elective, or in place of PE. This class would consist of a way to boost a person's self-confidence, how to stand up in an attack, hand to hand combat, survival skills, and what to keep on them in case of an attack. This class can fulfill the PE requirements of running and defense skills. This class would need the funds of an experienced self-defense teacher, equipment; dummies, mats, hand guards, and a protection suit for the “attacker”. Self-defense can also lower the rate of bullying at our school, many think that with the knowledge of self-defense, people will take advantage of fighting with skills, but many forget that the student being attacked now has the self-confidence to stand up for themselves.
