1. Shoes prevent foot problems like athlete’s foot. Shoes absorb the impact of walking, which prevents pain. The shoes protect against infections like hookworm. When walking barefoot, there is alway the risk of stepping on glass or nails.

2. The Sole Hope organization teaches families how to treat jiggers to stop infections. Sole Hope started by one person watching a video. It moved the person so much, they started the organization. 3. Wearing shoes keep your feet healthy and help stop back pain. Not wearing shoes causes people to get bacteria. Shoes also protect you from injuries like hidden rocks. Shoes also allow your feet to get a grip on slippery surfaces.

4. Children without shoes in Uganda get jiggers. It causes a lot of pain. When Sole Hope treats people in Uganda they also educate them on how to treat them themselves. 5. When people don’t have shoes they can get diseases. Shoes also open up jobs. Without shoes people can’t get some jobs that are more dangerous. Shoes prevent pains in the knee and feet. Shoes absorb the impact feet are not meant to take.

7. Jiggers are bugs the burrow in someone’s feet. They make walking impossible. The jiggers lay their eggs in their feet. After they are treated, they educated them on how to take care of themselves. 8. Shoes cushion landing when walking, running, and jumping. Shoes also prevent injuries like sprained ankles and fractures. Shoes also protect against Metatarsalgia which is pain in the balls of your feet.
